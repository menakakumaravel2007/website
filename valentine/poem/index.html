For You

enaku kavitha la theriyathu da ana onnu matum soltre ona enaku romba putikum da

evalavu vennu naalu sanda pottu pesama iru ana ena viitu matum poirathu da

ne epovum happya irukanum

apo apo tension panuve koncham poruthuko enakaka

finala ona romba miss pantre da

ena vittu matum poiratha da mama

I love you da mama and I miss you so much

Written from the heart, for you
